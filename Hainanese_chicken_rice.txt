Ingredients:
- 1 kg of chicken meat
- 2 liters of water
- a cup of sesame oil
- several cloves of chopped up garlic
- 1 1/2 liters of rice
- Salt

Directions:
-Stir fry the chopped up garlic in a pressure cooker using the sesame oil and then add the chicken. 
-Add the rice and water and salt.
-Close the lid of the pressure cooker and then heat until the pressure builds up. 
-Cook for about 5-7 minutes.
-Wait for the pressure cooker to cool down and enjoy the delicious rice.
